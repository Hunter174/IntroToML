ğŸ“· Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs)

CNNs are specialized for image and spatial data. Instead of connecting every neuron to every input pixel, they scan small regions (filters) to detect features like edges, corners, and patterns.

CNNs are used for:

  • Image classification (cats vs dogs)
  • Object detection (finding people in photos)
  • Medical imaging (detecting tumors)
